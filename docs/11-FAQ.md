# FAQ

Is this a hosted service?
- No. Wayfarer is self‑hosted. Each individual or organization deploys their own server and controls its URL and data.

Can I change my password or enable 2FA?
- Yes. Use account management to change password and enable two‑factor authentication.

Are trips and timelines public by default?
- No. Both are private by default. You must explicitly make a trip or your timeline public.

What data formats can I import/export?
- Import: GPX, KML, CSV, GeoJSON, Google Timeline JSON.
- Export: GeoJSON, CSV, GPX, KML (timeline) and PDF/KML for trips.

How do I join a group?
- By invitation from a group owner/manager. Accept in the app when invited.

Can I embed my timeline on my website?
- Yes. Use the public embed URL and place it in an iframe.
